## Overview

**uDOS** (Universal Data Operations System) is a free and open-source software project. We are committed to transparency, privacy, and respecting the intellectual property of others.

---

## Free and Open Source

- uDOS is **free for personal use**
- Source code is publicly available
- Licensed under the terms in [LICENSE.txt](LICENSE.txt)

---

## Not for Business Use

uDOS is provided for **personal, educational, and non-commercial use only**. It is not intended for business or commercial applications.

---

## No Data Collection

uDOS is an offline-first application:

- **No telemetry** — We don't track your usage
- **No analytics** — We don't collect data about you
- **No ads** — We don't monetize your attention
- **Local by default** — Your data stays on your machine

When using optional cloud services (Wizard module):
- API credentials are stored locally in `.env`
- We don't access or store your API keys
- You control all cloud interactions

## User Privacy

As an offline-first system:

- **Your files** remain on your local machine
- **Your commands** are not logged externally
- **Your data** is under your control

We encourage you to:
- Keep your API credentials secure
- Review code before running untrusted scripts
- Back up your important data
